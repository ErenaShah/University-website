About

South Central focuses on the study of technology, engineering, and related fields. We typically offer a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, and other areas. We have strong research programs in these fields. We are located in urban areas, near major technology companies. This allows students to have access to internships and job opportunities in the tech industry. We also tend to have strong financial aid programs, making us affordable for students from all backgrounds.

MISSION

To provide students with a world-class education that prepares them for success in a global society.

VALUES

Diversity, equity, and inclusion; academic excellence; civic engagement; and global citizenship.
