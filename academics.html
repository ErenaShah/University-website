Computer Science


The computer science program at South Central University prepares students for careers in software engineering, data science, and other in-demand fields. The program offers a rigorous curriculum that covers the fundamentals of computer science, as well as hands-on experience in programming, data analysis, and software development. Students also have the opportunity to participate in research projects and internships with leading companies in the tech industry.

Design


The design course at South Central University teaches students the fundamentals of design, such as typography, color theory, and composition. The course also covers the principles of visual communication, such as how to use design to convey a message. Students have the opportunity to work on a variety of design projects, such as posters, websites, and branding materials.
